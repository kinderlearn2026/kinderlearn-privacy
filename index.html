The short version: KinderLearn does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information — from children or anyone else. There are no accounts, no ads, no third-party analytics, and no internet connection required to use the app.

1. Who we are

KinderLearn is an educational app for children ages 3–6, covering letters, numbers, shapes, colors, sight words, days of the week, and nursery rhymes. It is developed and published by [Your Name / Company Name] ("we," "us," or "our").

2. Information we collect

We do not collect any information. Specifically, KinderLearn:

  • Does not require or offer account creation or sign-in
  • Does not collect names, emails, photos, or any personal identifiers
  • Does not use cookies, device identifiers, or advertising IDs
  • Does not use third-party analytics or tracking software development kits (SDKs)
  • Does not display advertising of any kind
  • Does not access contacts, location, camera, or microphone

3. How the app works

All learning content, progress tracking, spoken narration (via your device's built-in text-to-speech), and background music are generated and stored entirely on your device. Progress (such as which letters or numbers a child has tapped) is saved locally on the device only and is never transmitted to us or to any third party. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes this data.

4. Children's privacy (COPPA)

KinderLearn is designed for young children and is built to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent regulations in other regions, such as the EU's GDPR-K provisions and the UK's Age Appropriate Design Code. Because we do not collect any personal information from any user — child or adult — there is no personal data to request, correct, or delete. If this policy ever changes to include any form of data collection, we will update this policy in advance and, where required, obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting any information from children under 13.

5. Third parties

KinderLearn does not integrate with any third-party advertising networks, analytics providers, or data brokers. No data is sold, rented, or shared with any third party, because no data is collected in the first place.
